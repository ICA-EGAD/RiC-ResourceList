Semantic representation of the Registos de Baptismos da Paróquia de Aldoar (Porto, Portugal)

    • Lucia Giagnolini
    • Inês Koch
  • 2025-04-02

  • This dataset comprises mappings of archival records from the National Archives of Portugal to the RiC-O framework, namely the baptism registries of the Parish of Aldoar (Porto, Portugal) (PT/ADPRT/PRQ/PPRT01/001).

    Includes diagrams in draw.io.

    The RiC-O converter was used in a modified version to create the data.
