RiC-CM NavTool

  • Matthew Damigos (Laboratory of Digital Libraries and Electronic Publishing, Department of Archives, Library Science and Museology, Ionian University)
  • 2026

  • The RiC-CM NavTool is a web-based exploration tool developed by the Laboratory of Digital Libraries and Electronic Publishing, Department of Archives, Library Science and Museology, Ionian University, that enables users to browse, navigate, and understand the structure of the Records in Contexts Conceptual Model (RiC-CM). RiC-CM itself is a high-level archival metadata model designed to describe records, the agents involved (people or organizations), and the activities associated with them, along with the relationships between these entities.

  • https://dlib-ionian-university.github.io/ric-cm-nav/
